About the night

Short sets, steady pacing, and a room built for listening.

Open Mic Night is set up for guests who want to perform and guests who simply want a good seat, a cortado, and an easy way to spend a Thursday evening. We keep the set list moving, hold transitions between performers to a minimum, and leave enough breathing room for the night to feel warm instead of rushed.

Acoustic music, poetry, and spoken word all fit the format. We provide a house mic, a small PA, and a keyboard. Performers should plan on one short set, and listeners are welcome to come and go as the evening unfolds.

Schedule

How the evening is paced.

  1. 6:15 PM

    Doors open, the sign-up sheet comes out, and early guests can settle in with drinks.

  2. 6:45 PM

    House soundcheck and final lineup notes with the bar team and featured opener.

  3. 7:00 PM

    The first set begins, followed by a rotating lineup of short performances.

  4. 8:15 PM

    A quick midpoint pause for pastry restock, water refill, and room reset.

  5. 9:30 PM

    Final encore, thank-yous, and a gradual closeout with the room still open for goodbyes.

Before you come

A few house notes for a smoother night.

Acoustic sets and spoken-word pieces work best in the room. If you are bringing a small group, email us earlier in the week and we will do our best to hold a table near the back.

Street parking is usually easiest on Oak after 6:00 PM, and our evening menu includes drip coffee, espresso, tea, cookies, and two rotating pastry options.
